Meet The Director

The importance of education in our society cannot be over emphasized. It is the channel through which sound values of society are passed on from generation to generation. The vices of adulthood in our society today can be traced back to improper childhood upbringing. If we therefore, want an ideal society, we must be totally committed to providing the best education to the young ones of today. Our goal is to make childhood and learning a very memorable one for your wards. We do these by leaving permanent imprints on their minds through the various educational techniques and technology that we have employed. We are also providing the proper environment for the proper social and emotional development of your child. By so doing they become polished and complete adults with a balanced approach to life. Our commitment to excellence is unrivalled. We do not offer half measures in anything we do. We believe in the best, we expect the best and we give the best. Anything short of that is not good enough for the future of your child. We are also aware that education also involves training, not just teaching, we therefore provide the environment for pupils to practice what they are taught so that they can internalize them. I look forward to welcoming you to this new world of learning.

Aims and Objectives

Lodestar Academy aims to provide a structured, purposeful and caring learning environment for all its pupils, within which each girl or boy can feel confident to explore, develop and realise his or her full potential, helped and encouraged by teachers who are expert practitioners of their craft and fully commited to meting the needs of the children in their care.

We Aim To

  • Provide a caring and stimulating environment, which is safe and welcoming to all.
  • Provide a pleasant and challenging environment.
  • Nuture self-esteem in all, and so develop confidence and self-discipline
  • Value each child as a person, and help them to value and respect others.
  • Promote honesty, tolerance, cooperation and trust amongst adults and children, regardless of race gender or culture.

Results of Our Objectives

  • Children who are happy, open and sociable.
  • Children who tell the truth and take responsibility for their actions.
  • Children who are comfortable with adults and other pupils.
  • Children who adopt a "Can do" atitude, to use their initiative and who are not afraid to make mistakes.
  • Children who look after themselves, others, and their surroundings, both in and out of school.

Educational Aims

  • Deliver a child centered curriculum, which provides breadth, balance, relevance and progression.
  • Have high expectations for our children.
  • Build upon what each child knows and can do.
  • Develop the highest possible standards of literacy, numeracy and investigative skills in each student.
  • Develop children as independent and life long learners who can respond to the challenges of a fast changing world.
  • Move every child forward in small achievable steps, and recognise that assessment and target-setting are essential to this process.
  • Provide equal opportunities for all children.
  • Develop childrens creativity and aesthetic appreciation
  • Encourage children to care for their bodies through a personal, social and health education programme.
  • Promote an interest in and awareness of cultural diversity.
  • Work in partnership with parents for the good of each child.
  • Become involved with and involve the local community in the work of the school.

Facilities

At Lodestar Academy, your child gets a spacious play ground, well equipped computer laboratory, assorted stimulating learning aids, play/gaming equipments, well equipped health center, shuttle buses to/from school, and a childrens library among others.

Assembly

Assembly is an important part of school life and is taken seriously at Lodestar Academy. The children have assemblies on a weekly basis. the programme includes class assemblies, which are given by the pupils themselves.
